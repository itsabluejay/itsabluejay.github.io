HOW TO PLAY:

  • When the game starts, you'll have two and a half minutes to unscramble as many words as you can. Each word is seven letters long.

  • Type your guesses into the text box above the scrambled word. To submit them, press the 'enter' key on your keyboard, or click "Guess!". If you need a little help, you can click "Shuffle!" to reorder the letters.

  • Each correct guess will earn you one point. Each incorrect guess will cause you to lose one point. (Don't worry - your score can't drop below zero!)

  • When time runs out, you'll be given the opportunity to type in your name for the scoreboard. Clicking "Save score!" will update the high score table.
